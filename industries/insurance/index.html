Why It Matters

Carriers are raising
the bar — fast.

The insurance industry has seen a wave of cybersecurity incidents that has made carriers, wholesalers, and MGAs increasingly serious about the security posture of the agencies they work with. Many are now requiring documented controls as a condition of appointment or renewal.

At the same time, your clients trust you with their most sensitive personal information. A breach affecting client PII — SSNs, claims history, health data, financial records — carries notification obligations, regulatory exposure, and the kind of reputational damage that doesn't repair quickly in a relationship-based business.

The good news: the security controls that satisfy carriers are the same ones that protect your clients. You don't need two different programs — you need one that's properly documented and maintained.

"Your carriers want documentation. Your clients want confidence. We provide both."
Multi-Factor Authentication
Most carriers and E&O providers now require MFA on all systems handling client data. Agencies without it face coverage questions or appointment conditions.
Email Security Controls
Anti-phishing, email filtering, and impersonation protection. Agency email is a primary attack vector for both client fraud and carrier account compromise.
Verified Data Backup
Documented backup procedures with tested restore capability. Carriers and E&O underwriters ask whether backups are verified — "we assume so" isn't an answer.
Incident Response Plan
Written procedures for responding to a breach or ransomware attack. Many carriers require this documentation as a condition of cyber liability coverage.
Access Management & Offboarding
Documented procedures for granting and revoking access to agency management systems, carrier portals, and client data when staff changes occur.
What We Provide

IT and security for
how agencies operate.

Carrier-Ready Documentation

Written security policies covering MFA, access management, backup verification, and incident response — formatted to answer what your carriers, E&O underwriters, and MGAs are asking.

MFA Enforcement

Multi-factor authentication deployed and enforced across all agency systems — AMS, carrier portals, email, and cloud storage. Logged and documented for compliance purposes.

Email Security

Advanced phishing protection, business email compromise detection, and impersonation alerts. Agency email accounts are targeted specifically for carrier portal credential theft.

Backup & Recovery

Automated backup with monthly tested restores. Written documentation of backup procedures and verification results — exactly what carriers and E&O underwriters are asking to see.

Staff Onboarding & Offboarding

Documented access procedures for new hires and departing staff. When a producer leaves, their access to AMS, carrier portals, and client data is revoked immediately and documented.

24/7 Threat Monitoring

Managed endpoint detection and response. Threats are identified and contained automatically — not discovered after a client calls because their policy was changed fraudulently.
