Charlotte-local managed IT

Built for businesses that need technology to run securely, clearly, and without chaos.

AMP IT was founded by Nik Kotov and Mikhail Koval to help small and mid-size businesses in Charlotte modernize their technology, strengthen cybersecurity, and adopt AI without losing control of sensitive data or daily operations.

Built by operators with more than a decade in IT.

Nik Kotov, CEO of AMP Dynamic and Forbes 30 Under 30 / Y Combinator alum, is building AMP IT with Mikhail Koval, a Charlotte-based IT leader with more than a decade of hands-on technology experience.

Together, they saw a clear problem: small and mid-size businesses are adopting AI and new cloud tools quickly, but many do not have the guardrails, documentation, security controls, or support structure to do it safely.

Why AMP IT exists

Modern businesses rely on technology for almost everything: email, phones, files, client communication, billing, AI tools, security, and daily operations. But too many companies are forced to choose between slow enterprise IT, reactive break-fix support, or risky DIY systems.

AMP IT exists to give Charlotte businesses a better option: practical managed IT, clear cybersecurity, responsible AI governance, and support from people who understand that technology is only useful when the business can trust it.

Moving fast should not mean losing control.

AI is already inside most businesses, whether leadership has approved it or not. Employees use tools to summarize documents, draft emails, analyze data, and speed up daily work. That can be powerful — but without policies, access controls, and documentation, it can also expose client information, regulated data, and internal workflows.

AMP IT helps businesses adopt AI with confidence by building the foundation first: secure accounts, documented systems, clear policies, responsible workflows, and practical guardrails for Shadow AI and Shadow IT.

What we believe

Technology should make the business calmer, not more chaotic.
Cybersecurity should be practical, documented, and understandable.
AI adoption needs guardrails, not fearmongering.
Business owners deserve clear pricing and plain-English answers.
Good IT support should pick up the phone, document the work, and leave the client stronger than before.
